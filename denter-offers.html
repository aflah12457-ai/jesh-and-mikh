Frequently Asked Question

How often should I visit a dentist? −
Routine dental checkups every six months help maintain good oral health and prevent major dental problems.
What are early signs of dental problems? +
  • Tooth pain or sensitivity
  • Bleeding gums
  • Bad breath
  • Tooth discoloration
  • Loose teeth
When should a child visit a dentist first? +
A child's first dental visit should be around their first birthday or when the first tooth appears.
How much does dental treatment cost in Dubai? +
Treatment cost depends on the procedure. A consultation helps determine the exact treatment plan and pricing.
